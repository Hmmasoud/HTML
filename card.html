Scottish Fold Cat

Scottish Fold Cats are known for their folded ears due to mutation in their dominant-gene.

  • Origin: Scotland
  • Body: Medium-Size (9-13 lb.)
  • Coat: Long-hair and Shorthair
  • Health Risks: Low

This beautiful animal is a type of domestic cat that are human-oriented and strive for attention and care. They have wide big eyes that give them that extra owl-like look with a cute hint to it. I have one named "TOM".
